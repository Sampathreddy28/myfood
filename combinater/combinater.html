About The Cafe

Founded in 2024 by Sam, The Café began with a simple vision: to create a warm, inviting space where community and quality come together. From our earliest days, we've stayed true to that vision, serving exceptional coffee and wholesome food in a welcoming atmosphere. In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we proudly offer fresh, made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a carefully curated selection of sides, salads, and other delicious offerings. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or settling in with a latte, you'll find something to enjoy at The Café.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Sampath Reddy

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: 15 Address street, 502130, Medak
